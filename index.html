Our Story

  • Baptist Collegiate Ministries

    August 2013

    In the fall of 2013, two wide-eyed freshmen at the University of Florida started going to the BCM. Although they were around each other all the time at the BCM and at River Cross Church, they never really got to know each other. They simply saw each other around at events and hung out with their respective friends, not knowing that their futures would be so intertwined.

  • Fish School

    April 2014

    At the spring BCM Banquet, Hanna and Josh were paired together to lead a Fish School (freshman Bible study) during the following year. This seemed a bit strange to both of them, since they did not really know each other. During that year, they became close friends; leading a Bible study together necessitated that. They learned each other's likes and dislikes, their struggles, the things that really made them tick. It was a year filled with plenty of fun times, during which the hashtag #fishwifeforlife was born.

  • BCM Leadership Retreat

    August 2015

    After spending an entire summer apart, Hanna in Portland and Josh in Orlando, the two were reunited at the BCM Leadership Retreat in August. This was a time where the BCM leadership team would plan the events for the entire next year; Hanna was the new President, and Josh was one of the Freshman Team leaders. While catching up after a summer on different sides of the country, Josh began to develop feelings for Hanna. These feelings grew even stronger after talking out on the beach into the early hours of the morning.

  • First Date

    September 2015

    After some time had gone by, and lots of praying and talking had been done by both Josh and Hanna, it was finally time for the first date. Josh, knowing how much Hanna loved tacos, took Hanna out to La Tienda, a Mexican restaurant local to Gainesville. After a delicious meal, they continued home to a dessert of chocolate covered strawberries and enjoyed a movie together. After Josh took Hanna home, he knew that he had found someone truly special, the thought of which made him one of the happiest fellas on the planet that night.

  • Falling in Love

    Late 2015

    After many more dates (and even more nights spent inside studying together), Josh and Hanna began to fall in love. They worked at desks right next to each other, helped lead worship at church on Sunday mornings, and helped on the executive team at the BCM. When Josh broke his foot in November, Hanna did all she could to take care of him, showing him the true love that she had for him. As they began to talk more and more about the possibility of marriage, Josh began to devise a plan...

  • Engagement

    February 29, 2016

    On Leap Day 2016, that plan was put into action. Josh had been giving Hanna clues to their "Great Date" in the form of websites (like this one) for weeks, trying to make her think there was no way he would propose on a day he had told her so much about. After a meal at a Mexican restaurant and a walk through the park, they decided to take a stroll along the bay on the way to a fancy dinner. During the walk, they came across something written on the ground that Josh pretended to know nothing about, although he himself had written it earlier that day.

    "Take a Leap" it said, in large letters. Hanna took the first jump and Josh marked it on the ground. As Josh got ready to jump, Hanna reminded him that he couldn't, as he was still recovering from a broken foot. "You're right," Josh responded. "So I'll take a leap of a different kind." With that, Josh got down on one knee and asked Hanna to be his wife. Ten months and one day later, that will finally become a reality!
