#fishwifeforlife - The Story Behind the Hashtag


As most of you know, our wedding hashtag is #fishwifeforlife. What some of you may not know, however, is why that was chosen - and no, it's not because we love to go fishing together. At the end of our freshman year at the BCM, Hanna and I were paired together to lead a freshman Bible study, which is called a "Fish school". The leaders are referred to as the "fish mom" and "fish dad;" hence, Hanna was my fish wife.

The night we were paired together, I posted this picture with the hashtag, simply thinking it was funny and kind of catchy, not realizing what it would turn into. I continued to use the hashtag whenever I posted pictures of the two of us, just for the sake of consistency. After we got engaged, it just seemed too perfect; all of our pictures together already shared a common hashtag. It's such an amazing blessing from God that this young woman who I grew close to while teaching God's Word will now actually become my real wife!

While you're enjoying your time at our wedding, we would love for you to snap a picture and post it to some sort of social media with this hashtag! This way it will be easy for us to see all the fun things that people got up to on our big day. We're so excited to have you join us as we take this next step together!
